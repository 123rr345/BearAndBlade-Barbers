About BEAR&BLADE

Welcome to BEAR&BLADE — where traditional barbering meets modern style. Nestled in the heart of Wheathampstead, our shop is built on a simple belief: every man deserves a proper cut in a space that feels like his own.

Step inside and you'll find deep burgundy walls, gold-trimmed barber chairs, and a leather chesterfield where you can settle in before your appointment. It's the kind of place you want to come back to.

Our barbers are skilled in everything from clean skin fades to classic scissor cuts and hot towel shaves. We take our time, get the detail right, and send you out looking sharp.
